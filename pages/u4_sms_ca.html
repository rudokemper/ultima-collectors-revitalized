Description

Ultima IV for the Mastersystem is the first (and so far the only) Ultima that was ported to a Sega game console. It seems that most (if not all) of these cartridges were produced for the European market, they contain a multi-lingual (English, French and German) manual, both books from the original version as well as a folded paper map. The Canadian edition came with two sets of books attached to the package - one English, the other French.
