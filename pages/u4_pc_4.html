Description

In preparation of the 10th anniversary of the series Ultima IV was rereleased for the IBM PC platform around 1989. Some copies of this edition may contain a different Book of Mystic Wisdom. It's cover is dark brown, almost black with more or less white writing on it (instead of the usual red / golden); the History of Britannia book typically has the dark brown sleeve with the newer Londonderry address on the back. This particular edition came on double-sided, single-density 3.5" disks and had a slightly modified Player Reference Card.
