Description

The first editions of Ultima III had a quite unique box design consisting of two covers made of folded hard paper with very glossy surface. This second release is still missing the Origin logo on the front but has no address printed on the back side. The mail address on the reference card (orange on grey, old style layout) as well cluebook flyers of these editions is North Andover MA 01845. Later prints of Ultima III may contain remnants from previous releases so mix ups are quite common!
