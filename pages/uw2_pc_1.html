Description

Just a year after the first Underworld game, Origin released a sequel which had improved graphics, a more advanced spell system and was better integrated into the current main series plot. Storywise it is therefore completely unrelated to the first Underworld game - on the Ultima timeline it takes place about one year after the destruction of the Black Gate (Ultima 7 Pt. 1) and before the Avatar is being send to the Serpent Isle (Ultima 7 Pt. 2).
