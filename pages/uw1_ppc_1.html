Description

Korean publisher Zio Interactive ported Ultima Underworld to the Pocket PC handheld platform in 2002. The game is pretty much identical with the original PC version with just some minor tweaks and changes to overcome the limitations of the device (such as an on-screen keyboard). Additional printable documentation (Memoirs of Sir Cabirus, Players Guide) was available for download from the ZioSoft web site.
