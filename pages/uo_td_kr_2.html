Description

Electronic Arts Korea produced a special promotional package of Ultima Online: Third Dawn in collaboration with local pop group 'Juju Club'. The package contained the latest CD of the band and also featured their cover artwork (with the singer wearing UO necklace and ear rings) on the front of the box.
