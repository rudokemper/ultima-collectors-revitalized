Description

The Japanese 'Ultima Complete' is so far the most complete compilation of the Ultima series. It contains the japanese 'Ultima Collection' plus Ultima IX plus Ultima Online Lord Blackthorn's Revenge Edition. It also has a number of unique trinkets, like a Britannia Citizenship Certificate, a metal virtue coin as well as one trading card from the Ultima Generation set.
