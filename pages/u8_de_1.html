Description

The European releases of Ultima VIII came in typical EA style packages consisting of a black box with separate cover sleeve. The design was the same for all editions (disk, CD) - pentagram on front cover, just like the US disk release. The game itself was fully localized for France, Germany and UK including manuals, ingame speech (or separate speech packs). The overall print quality of the documentation and especially the map was poor (and also of slightly different make).
