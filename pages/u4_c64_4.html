Description

This rare special edition of Ultima IV for the C64/128 was distributed by US Gold in the UK and some other European countries only. It comes in a small (6x8.5" / 15x21 cm) flip-up plastic box that contains smaller versions of the original manuals, a paper print of the original 1st generation map and 2 5.25" disks. There's no ankh included. The German Edition contains a translated Reference Card (Spielerkarte).
