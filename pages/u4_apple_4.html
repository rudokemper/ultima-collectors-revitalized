Description

Korean hard and software distributor Topia apparently released this edition of Ultima IV as part of their Apple II software catalogue sometime in the late 1980s. The package consists of a flip-up plastic box with Korean game manual and disks without any additional bonus material. Genuineness of this item is still not verified.
