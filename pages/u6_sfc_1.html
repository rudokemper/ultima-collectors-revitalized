Description

Considering the limitations of the system (and the targeted audience), Ultima VI runs surprisingly well on the SuperNintendo Console (SuperFamicom in Japan). There are of course some simplifications due to lack of proper input controls and some of the dialogue was modified but all in all this port is very close to its computer counterpart. Unfortunately where the game itself shines the documentation is lacking severely - a simple instruction booklet is all the player gets.
