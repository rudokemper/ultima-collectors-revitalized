Description

Only very few titles in computer gaming history revolutionized the industry as much as Ultima Underworld did. Before there was Doom and its myriad of clones and massmarket compatible action shooters, a small company named 'Looking Glass' developed a 3D engine that not only allowed free movement in all directions but also attempted to combine that with traditional roleplaying features.
