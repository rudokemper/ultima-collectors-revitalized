Description

A second edition of 'California Pacific Computers' Akalabeth contained a new type of cover sheet featuring artwork from Denis Loubet which Richard Garriott found on the cover of an issue of 'Space Gamer' Magazine ('Wrong Number', Issue 28). There's also a completely new game manual included.
