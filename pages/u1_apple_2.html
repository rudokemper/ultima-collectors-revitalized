Description

California Pacific Computers re-released Ultima in 1981 under a different (maybe low-budget?) distribution label. For this edition the separate cover art page was removed and the artwork was used for the Play Book instead (which is otherwise identical with the original). Both, disk as well as manual feature the 'Progame' logo.
