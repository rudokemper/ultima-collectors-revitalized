Description

The first ports of Ultima games onto japanese 8-Bit homecomputers were made by a company named Starcraft. While the games itself seem to be similar to the Apple II original (only very limited Japanese translation) the packaging and content is very different. The box is small, just the size of a 5.25" disk and contains fully translated manuals as well as a cloth map. There are different versions for the PC9801 series, on 5.25" or 3.5" disks.
