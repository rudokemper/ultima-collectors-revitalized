Description

Later editions of Ultima IV for the Apple II system contained a new type of cloth map made of more sturdy, cotton-like material and slightly different in design. The print was more blueish and less blurry and of overall better quality than the original map and at least some editions are missing the 'Cape of Heroes' writing on the lower part. Other than the map and the sticker on the box everything, including reference card remained the same with this release.
