Description

When Sierra Online started porting Ultima II to the various homecomputer systems they also re-released Richard Garriott's original Ultima game under their 'Sierraventure' label. The only known version of this release is the one for the Atari 8 bit computers (apparently the partnership with Sierra ended before any other conversions could be made). It was named 'Ultima I - The Original' and featured the artwork from the Ultima II back cover on the front.
