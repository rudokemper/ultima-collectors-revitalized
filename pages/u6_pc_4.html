Description

Later Editions of Ultima VI were released as 'Special Promotional Version' in the United States and 'Original Uncut Version' in Europe. Of course there's nothing really 'special' or even 'uncut' in these prints - they don't have the Moonstone trinket and cloth map, just the game on HD disks and some documentation.
