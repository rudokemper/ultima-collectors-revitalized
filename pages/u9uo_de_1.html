Description

The 'Ultima World Edition' or 'Special Limited Gamers Edition' is a combo package consisting of the German versions of Ultima IX and UO: The Second Age. It is basically both original boxes bundled in a separate sleeve cover showing the Ultima IX artwork on one side and the Second Age motive on the other. The content of these boxes is identical with the German retail editions of the games.
