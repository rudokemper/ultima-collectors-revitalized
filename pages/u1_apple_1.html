Description

The original Ultima was distributed by California Pacific Computers for the Apple II in 1981. Like Akalabeth and many other games back then it was released in a simple ziploc bag containing the disk, a play book, reference card and a nice 'cover' art page - amazingly they still sold about 50.000 copies this way. This first release has the line 'top of the orchard software' printed on both the cover as well as the disk.
