Description

Amongst most fans and critics, Ultima VII is regarded as one of the best roleplaying games of all times. Not only is it a truly epic adventure and immersive gameworld - it's also the first (and only) part of the series that spawned its own sequels... A re-release of the original US edition had a game review page of the PC Games magazine attached to its back side. This particular version also contained the second print of the cloth map like the Serpent Isle Ultima 7 Complete or European EA re-issues.
