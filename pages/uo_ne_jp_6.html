Description

In order to attract new players EA Games released their UO: Age of Shadows a second time as a full version 'Ultima Online' package. The small cardboard box contains just a thin manual, the two maps as well as an additional 'Story Book'. Only the first 5000 copies came with an UO 'snap hook' with integrated LED light (availabe in 11 different color/light variations and indicated by a special shrinkwrap insert).
