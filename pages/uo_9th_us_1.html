Description

The Ultima Online 9th Anniversary Collection is a compilation of all 7 expansion packs commemorating the 9th anniversary of Ultima Online. Like the '8th Age' it is primarily aimed at new customers who receive a bonus of 30 free days of gameplay. The game client is based on the latest 'Mondains Legacy' edition. Special features of this particular edition are a set of new housing styles and various types of 'servants' to interact with. The double-size dvd case contains a small 15 pages booklet and a Trial DVD of EA's latest online game 'Dark Age of Camelot'.
