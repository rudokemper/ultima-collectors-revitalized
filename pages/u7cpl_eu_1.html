Description

The European editions of the first 'Complete Ultima VII' compilation contained the same original manuals and maps like the US release (more precisely, the original manuals from their respective language edition) - earlier packages even contained the Ultima VII cloth map. The box was of the usual European EA design (separate box and sleeve).
