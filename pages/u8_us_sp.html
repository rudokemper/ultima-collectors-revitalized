Description

Ultima VIII was initially released on 3.5" disks that did not contain the complete game - the speech for some of the characters could be purchased separately as a so called 'speech pack'... Considering that the CD edition that contained all that in 3 different languages was distributed at the same time for almost the same price this seemed to be a questionable 'update'.
