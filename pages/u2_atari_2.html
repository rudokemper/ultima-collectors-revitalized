Description

A quite uncommon release of the 'big box' Ultima II version for the Atari 8 Bit systems. Box design, manual and cloth map remained the same as the other editions but it has a separate fullsize Player Guide Sheet and Reference Card just like the IBM and later Apple versions. There's also an 'Errata Sheet' which is identical to the Player Guide...
