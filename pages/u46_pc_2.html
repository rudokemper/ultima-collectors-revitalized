Description

The 'Second Trilogy' compilation consisting of Ultima IV - VI was released in 1992. The box was of Ultima VI-like dimensions and came with all in all 7 disks (3.5" low density) and a single book containing most (but not all) of the documentation from the original games. The included map is of the same type as the cardboard map from the Ultima VI Amiga version and later PC re-releases, it just reads 'The Second Trilogy - Ultima IV-VI' instead.
