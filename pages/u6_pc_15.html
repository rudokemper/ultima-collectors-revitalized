Description

Ultima VI was the first game of the series that fully utilized the audio-visual capabilities of "modern" PCs. It supported VGA mode with 256 colors and various kinds of soundcards. The European package contained two separate Reference Guides, a French and an English one plus the customary cloth map and trinket (an 'orb of the moons' made of black glass and of varying size).
