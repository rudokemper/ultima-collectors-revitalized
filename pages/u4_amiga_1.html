Description

The Amiga version of Ultima IV was released more than 2 years late but is pretty much identical to the other releases - content wise. The only real differences are the disks (obviously) and the reference card. The game itself however has been significantly improved for both 16 bit systems (Amiga and ST). It features more colorful graphics, the complete soundtrack of the original Apple & C64 releases and has mouse support.
