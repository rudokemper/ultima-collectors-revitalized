Description

A release by Korean hard and software distributor 'Soft Bank'. The sturdy cardboard package resembles the international release and seems to contain at least some of its original documentation like a translated version of the 'History of Britannia' booklet. Whether there was also a map or additional material included remains unclear at the moment.
