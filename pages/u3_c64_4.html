Description

A special version of Ultima III was released in the UK by US Gold under their 'All American Adventures' label. The box is rather small and flimsy (about 4/5 of the standard size) and contains smaller versions of the original manuals plus a black and white copy of the Exodus map. There are C64 and Atari versions.
