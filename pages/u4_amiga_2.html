Description

This special release of Ultima IV was distributed by Microprose in Europe only. It comes in a significantly smaller box (6x8.5" / 15x21 cm) that contains similarly shrinked manuals which are otherwise identical with their original counterparts. This edition always contains the 3rd generation cloth map - clear, blueish print on a sturdy material as well as the metal ankh.
