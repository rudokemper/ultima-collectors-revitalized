Description

In 1989, Ponycanyon released a remake of Ultima III for a number of japanese computer systems. Just like the previous releases of Ultima I,II and IV it was distributed in the usual flip-up plastic box that contained the original set of manuals (translated) as well as the cloth map (very different from the original).
