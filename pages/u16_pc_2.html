Description

Basically, the Ultima I-VI Series CDs are just the two previous trilogy releases combined into one. They contain all the maps and manuals, only the software is on CD instead separate disks. It should be noted that as a side effect, Ultima II got broken and is missing vital parts from the original Galactic Disk. (this has not been corrected on any of the following CD compilations since).
