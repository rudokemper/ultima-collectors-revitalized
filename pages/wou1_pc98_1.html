Description

The Savage Empire was the last game in the series of Ultima releases from Japanese distributor PonyCanyon. Unlike previous games it came in a standard cardboard box (same size as the US original) that contained the fully translated 'Ultimate Adventures' booklet as well as the paper map (same as those found in the original IBM PC edition).
