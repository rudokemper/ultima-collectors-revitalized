Description

The Ultima Collection CD contains the same game versions as the Ultima I-VI series before (that is including the non-working Ultima II) plus the complete Ultima VII and Ultima VIII and a couple of videos featuring Richard Garriott explaining the Ultima timeline. As a bonus they included a DOS version of Richard Garriotts first game 'Akalabeth'. The Documentation is completely on CD apart from the installation pamphlet and an 'Atlas' with black and white maps of all Ultimas. The Australian edition comes in standard box without flip cover like its US or Euro counterpart.
