Description

The Asia Pacific Edition of Ultima Online: Age of Shadows was published by Electronic Arts Australia for the English language market of the East Asian / Oceanic / Pacific region (mainly Australia). The rather big package contains almost identical printed documentation as the US release (but everything including CD printed in Singapore) plus an additional registration card from EA Australia.
