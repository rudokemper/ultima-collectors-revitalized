Description

Ultima III for the Atari ST was initially released around 1986. It is very similar to the re-releases for other systems as far as box design and content is concerned. However, the game itself has been improved quite a bit for this version, the graphics are more colorful, there's mouse support and it also comes with the complete soundtrack.
