Description

The Japanese as well as the European Editions of Ultima Online: Third Dawn have been released in DVD type packages - they only contain a thin booklet explaining the basics of the game as well as a small glossy paper map and the CD. There is an Upgrade and a Full (New Player) Edition of the Japanese Third Dawn - the New Player version has free gametime for the first 30 days and the necessary codes to create a new account.
