Description

Ultima V for the Apple II platform was certainly a rather rare sight in Europe considering the insignificance of that system on that market. Even more astonishing in that context is this particular release distributed by Microprose around 1989. It comes in a significantly smaller box (6x8.5" / 15x21 cm) that contains shrinked manuals which are otherwise identical with their original counterparts. Interestingly, the player reference card is even smaller than usual for these editions... Cloth map and coin are also included.
