Description

The first editions of Ultima III for Atari 8bit home computers had a quite unique box design consisting of two covers made of folded hard paper with very glossy surface. Earlier prints still have the Houston,TX address printed on the back - later runs either have a sticker taped over or nothing at all. The mail address on these stickers, on the reference card (dark blue on grey, old style layout) as well cluebook flyers of these editions is North Andover MA 01845. Later prints of Ultima III may contain remnants from previous releases so mix ups are quite common!
