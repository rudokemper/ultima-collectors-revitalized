Description

In 1986 Origin rewrote the original Ultima, improved its graphics and playability and released it as 'Ultima I - The First Age of Darkness' for a number of systems. The packaging and design is very similar to their other releases of that time - it comes with separate cardboard maps of the four continents and a bag with some 'Coins of the Realm' (1 Gold, 3 Silver and 1 Copper).
