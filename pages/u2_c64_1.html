Description

In late 1983 Sierra started to use a simpler and much smaller (just about 14x19 cm / 5.5x7.5") box design for many of their budget titles, also for the IBM and C64 port and an Atari 8 bit re-release of Ultima II. This new package contains pretty much the same as the later 'Grey Box' editions but also still the large cloth map.
