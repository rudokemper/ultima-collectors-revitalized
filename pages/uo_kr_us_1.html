Description

Ultima Online 'Kingdom Reborn' was a major update of the client side graphics engine and game interface. It was designed by EA Mythic (formerly Mythic Entertainment) and was offered as a free download from the developers website. A free backup DVD (plus shipping and handling fee) was available for a limited time per online order form.
