Description

In 1994 EA began to re-release many of the older Origin titles such as the Ultima Underworld compilation under their 'EA Classics' label. The box is of the same size and make like the Ultima VII Add-ons (about 17 x 22 cm / 6.5 x 8.5") and only contains the CD and a small pamphlet with installation instructions.
