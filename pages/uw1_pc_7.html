Description

Ultima Underworld was one of the last Ultimas distributed in Europe by Mindscape and it came already in the new EU standard sized box. Even though the box says otherwise the game's manual was not multilingual, only the installguide had French, Italian and German sections. The box content is more or less the same as the US original (including the bag with runes), only the manuals are slightly different in size and a bit more glossy print.
