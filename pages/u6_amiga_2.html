Description

Ultima VI was the last part of the series for the Commodore Amiga system. Its release was delayed for more than a year and in the end it was not really playable with the standard equipment. The package contains a cardboard print (same as those included in some of the compilations) instead of a cloth map and multilingual reference cards for the Amiga as well as IBM PC version.
