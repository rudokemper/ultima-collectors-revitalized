Description

The Ponycanyon releases of Ultima IV are pretty much identical for all systems, they all contain the 3rd generation type cloth map as well as fully translated manuals and come in the same flip-up plastic box. The MSX version differs a bit because of its inferior graphical capabilities - the low resolution prevents the use of full Kanji.
