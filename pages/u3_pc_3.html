Description

The last editions of Ultima III for the IBM PC were released around 1987. The only visible differences to previous re-releases are the sticker on the front, the reference card, the new style cluebook ad (it now mentions the address in Londonderry, NH) as well as the included cloth map which is of slightly different, synthetic make and is less colorful than the original.
