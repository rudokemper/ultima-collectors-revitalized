Description

Quite some time after the official Ultima Online updates Third Dawn and Lord Blackthorn's Revenge EA re-released the old Renaissance 2D Client as a separate 'Special Value' pack in their CD-ROM Classics line. It's a simple jewel case release with instructions booklet and CD - the rest of the documentation is on CD.
