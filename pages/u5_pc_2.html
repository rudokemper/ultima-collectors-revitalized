Description

Later editions of the IBM PC version as well as all European distributions from Mindscape were released in the 'C' type box that had the Origin logo centered on the bottom of the front and featured a series of 3 screenshots taken from that particular version on the backside (a rat in a dungeon, Britanny Bay and a battle scene).
