Description

Ultima: Runes of Virtue II was ported to the Super Nintendo / Super Famicom console as 2nd game in a series of Ultima conversions for this system in 1994. Gameplay wise it is identical with the Gameboy version but it got a (obviously) vastly improved graphics and sound set. Both packages, Japanese as well as US edition do not contain any other documentation apart from the instruction booklet.
