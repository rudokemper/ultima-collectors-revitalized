Description

The Japanese Ultima Collection was published by LOCUS, the company who made a couple of hint books and the 'Genesis of Ultima' compilation before. The CD contains PC98 disk images for Ultima I-VI (and the necessary emulator to use them on a Windows PC), the English version of the Complete Ultima VII as well as Ultima VIII for Japanese DOS/V. There's a solid hand book covering all games and as a bonus a set of 9 cards featuring the original (US) cover artwork of each game (only included with the First Limited Edition, indicated by a red sticker on the box).
