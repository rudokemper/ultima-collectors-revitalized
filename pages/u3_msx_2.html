Description

Ultima: Exodus for the cartridge slot of the japanese MSX-2 is a rare port of the Nintendo version published by FCI/Ponycanyon. Unlike the Famicom release it came in a solid videotape like box (made of white plastic and of about the same size as the Ponycanyon computer releases) that only contained the cartridge and the 40 pages manual. Gameplay-wise it is more or less identical with the Famicom edition.
