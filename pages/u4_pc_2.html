Description

Ultima IV for the IBM PC platform was released quite a while after the 8 Bit home computer versions. It contained the original set of manuals (light brown History of Britannia Book) and extras (New type cloth map and Metal Ankh). This particular edition came on double-sided, single-density 3.5" disks and had a slightly modified Player Reference Card.
