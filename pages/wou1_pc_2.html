Description

Based on the Ultima VI game engine Origin created a new series of spin-offs which they labeled 'Worlds of Ultima'. The idea behind it was that an Orb of the Moons allows the Avatar to summon moongates that could lead basically everywhere in time and space. Savage Empire was the first in this line and took place on Eodon, a prehistoric land covered with dense jungle and populated by primitive tribes, obscure animals, even dinosaurs... The European low density disk edition contains a multilingual 'Addendum Card' with installation instructions in English, French and German.
