Description

Ultima Underworld is the first (and only) Ultima for the Sony Playstation and is only available for Japanese systems. It is a complete remake for that video console that features new 3D graphics, sounds and ingame speech. The package is a standard PSX jewelcase with just the CD-ROM and small instruction booklet.
