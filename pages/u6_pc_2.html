Description

People who preordered Ultima VI received a 'Special Edition' that contained an additional audio-cassette (45 min) featuring Richard Garriott elaborating about the Ultima series, an allegedly 'hand-selected' moonstone as well as some sort of certificate. This certificate as well as the box front cover were signed by Lord British and also Dennis Loubet, back then chief artist at Origin.
