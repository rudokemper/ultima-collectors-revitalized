Description

The Spanish edition of Ultima VII Part 2 was produced and distributed by local publisher DROSoft. All printed documentation, packaging and the game itself was fully localized. It contains a paper map and a single black & white manual. The box design resembles the EU standard releases (separate sleeve and box).
