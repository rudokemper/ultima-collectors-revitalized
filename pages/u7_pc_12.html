Description

Ultima VII was the first Ultima that became fully localized for the European market (there are English, German and French editions), including manuals, ingame speech and even the game's title on the box (Die Schwarze Pforte). The package is of the new EU standard size and contains modified manuals (size and print), a cloth map and the Fellowship medaillon. Later editions were already released by Electronic Arts and contained a new set of disks, the US style cloth map (second print), manuals with new address and a multilingual Player Reference Card.
