Description

In late 1983 Sierra began to use a simpler and much smaller (just about 14x19 cm / 5.5x7.5") box design for many of their budget titles such as the re-release of Ultima II for for the Apple II, IBM, C64 and Atari 8 bit platforms. The content of this new package is pretty much identical to the later 'Grey Box' editions but some items can vary randomly. The Apple II edition usually contains the large cloth map with new Sierra logo and a new style registration card.
