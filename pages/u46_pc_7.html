Description

The European Editions of The 'Second Trilogy' compilation were distributed by Mindscape. Just like other releases (i.e. Underworld, Ultima VII) the box was somewhat smaller than the Origin US release. However, other than an additional multi-lingual Player Reference Card the content was identical. There was no 'Special Edition' with cloth maps etc though.
