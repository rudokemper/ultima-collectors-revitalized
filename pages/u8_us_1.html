Description

Initially, Ultima VIII was released on 3.5" disks. It was the only US-Edition that featured the original cover artwork created by Dennis Loubet with the large pentagram on flames. It contained a colorful cloth map printed on some nylon-type material and an aluminium pentagram coin as additional trinket.
