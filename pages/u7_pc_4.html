Description

Ultima VII was the first Ultima that became fully localized for the European market (there are English, German and French editions), including manuals, ingame speech and even the game's title on the box (La Portre Noir). The package is of the new EU standard size and contains modified manuals (size and print), a new type of cloth map with a thick yellow border around it (and of rather mediocre print quality) and the Fellowship medaillon.
