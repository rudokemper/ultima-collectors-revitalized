Description

This Special Edition of Savage Empire was only available for people who pre-ordered the game. It has an autographed box and contains an additional T-Shirt (white, featuring dinosaur artwork and the slogan 'I conquered the Savage Empire') plus the official Savage Empire hint book. The rest of the package is identical with the regular retail version.
