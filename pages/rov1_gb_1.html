Description

The Runes of Virtue series consists of 2 spin-offs developed by Origin exclusively for the Nintendo Gameboy handheld console. Even though they are labeled as 'Ultima' and feature some characters and location from the main series no one should expect some sort of epic roleplaying here as those games are more or less just simple, Zelda-like adventure games where the player can solve puzzles and collect stuff in order to defeat the end boss (some 'Black Knight' in this case) - but then, its for the Gameboy after all...
