Description

The Ultima Value Pak was a special bundle of the Ultima Trilogy, Ultima IV and Ultima V distributed exclusively at 'Software Etc.' stores. It consists of all three original packages wrapped in a separate card board sleeve. Each of the boxes may have a marking identifying it as part of a bundle.
