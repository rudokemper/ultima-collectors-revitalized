Description

The Ponycanyon releases of Ultima IV are pretty much identical for all systems, they all contain the 3rd generation type cloth map as well as fully translated manuals and come in the same flip-up plastic box. The gameplay of the 8 bit versions, X1, FM-7 as well as PC8801, is very similar to their 16 bit counterparts - there are just some minor differences due to lower screen resolution (640x200 instead 640x400) and sound modulation.
