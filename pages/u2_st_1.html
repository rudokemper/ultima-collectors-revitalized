Description

The Atari ST version of Ultima II was published in 1985 (even though the box claims otherwise) by Sierra in the same type of box as the re-releases for PC or Apple. It has a slightly different manual that also covers the Macintosh port and *usually* contains a much smaller variation of the cloth map (40x30 cm/ 16x12"). This version is fully integrated into the mouse driven GEM interface of the Atari ST and thus differs considerably gameplay wise. There is only one box variation for the Atari ST version (A+ Magazine quote on the back).
