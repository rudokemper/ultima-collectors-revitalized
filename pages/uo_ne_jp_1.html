Description

Under the 'New Ultima Online' label EA Japan released budget versions that don't contain the 3D client software introduced with UO: Third Dawn. This first edition is still based on the older UO: Renaissance and thus not offer any features of the later retail releases. It has an additional 'Guide Book' with relevant trivia and instructions for new users.
