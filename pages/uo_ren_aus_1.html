Description

UO: Renaissance is the third official release of the popular MMORPG. It features twice the landmass of the original as well as all recent additions and patches. Players who already have one of the previous installations don't need to buy this one to take advantage of the new features - they can update the game online. Renaissance comes with a detailed Player's Guide but none of the trinkets from the original version. The two maps are part of the nicely printed reference guide.
