Description

Ultima V for the Amiga was released about 2 years after the Apple Original. The box design and content is identical with the IBM PC 'C' version but the game itself differs a bit. While it is graphically very similar (apart from the big 'Ultima V' logo occupying the lower left half of the screen) it does not contain the original soundtrack like previous releases but just a single continous background melody.
